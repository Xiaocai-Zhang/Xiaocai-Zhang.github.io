ABOUT ME

Dr. Xiaocai Zhang is currently a Research Scientist at the Institute of High Performance Computing, Agency for Science, Tachnology and Research (A*STAR), Singapore. He receieved his B.E. and M.E. degrees in Traffic & Transport Enginerring both at the College of Navigation, Dalian Maritime University (DMU) in 2013 and 2016, respectively, and then his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at the Advanced Analytics Institute, Faculty of Engineering and Information Technology, University of Technology Sydney (UTS) in 2021. From Feb. 2019 to Dec. 2020, he worked at the Data Science Institute of UTS, as a Research Assistant with A/Prof. Yang Wang and Prof. Fang Chen. His current research foucus on time-sereis analysis, spatio-temporal mining for transport networks, deep-learning based intelligent transport systems, etc.
